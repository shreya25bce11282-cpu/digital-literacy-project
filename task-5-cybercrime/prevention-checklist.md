# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never click links in emails or SMS claiming to be from your bank. Always go to the website directly
2. Enable two factor authentication (2FA) on all accounts
3. Check the URL carefully before entering login details. Look for https:// and verify the domain name
4. Do not download attachments from unknown senders
5. Use a strong, unique password for every account
6. Regularly check your bank statements for unrecognized transactions
7. Keep your phone OS and apps updated. Updates fix security vulnerabilities
8. Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking or logging into important accounts

### UPI Safety (Mandatory Tips)
9. **[UPI]** Never share your UPI PIN with anyone. Banks and payment apps never ask for it
10. **[UPI]** Set a daily UPI transaction limit in your banking app to minimize potential losses

### Reporting
- Report any cybercrime at: cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7)
