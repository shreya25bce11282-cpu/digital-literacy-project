# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ DO's
1. Keep your LinkedIn profile updated and professional
2. Use strong, unique passwords for each social media account
3. Think before you post. Ask "would I be comfortable if my professor saw this?"
4. Report cyberbullying or harassment using platform tools
5. Use privacy settings to control who sees your personal information

## ❌ DON'Ts
1. Don't share your location, college ID, or phone number publicly
2. Don't post during conflicts or when emotional. Wait 24 hours
3. Don't accept friend requests from people you don't know
4. Don't engage with or forward unverified news or rumours
5. Don't use the same password across multiple platforms
